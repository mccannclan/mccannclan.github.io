Aunt Het's Butter Cookies

So pretty topped with a small mound of sprinkles in the middle of each cookies I don't know who Aunt Het is, just as I'm sure you might not know who Aunt Katie and Aunt Wishie are!

Ingredients

  • 1 c sugar
  • 1 sm egg
  • 1/2 t vanilla
  • 1/2 t almond extract
  • 2&1/4 c flour
  • 1 c butter
  • 1 t soda
  • dash nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Mix all together. Shape into 2 rolls. Each roll will fit into a small frozen orange juice can. Refrigerate Slice and place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes, or until just barely brown. Quickly top with a half a pecan or sprinkles.
