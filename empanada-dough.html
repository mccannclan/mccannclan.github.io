Empanada Dough

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 5 tbsp melted butter (or margarine)
  • 5 tbsp water (The amount of water used varies depending on the humidity where you live)

Instructions:

  • 1) Stir all ingredients until fully mixed.
  • 2) Knead the dough just enough to bring dough together.
  • 3) Form a round ball, cover, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  • 4) Roll out dough and cut into circles of desired size.
