Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting

Blane's Favorite Birthday Cake

Ingredients

  • CAKE:
  • 2 c flour
  • 2 c sugar
  • 2 t baking soda
  • 1 t salt
  • 3 t cinnamon
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 c corn oil
  • 4 c finely grated carrots
  • FROSTING:
  • 8 oz soft cream cheese
  • 4 T butter
  • 2 c powdered sugar
  • 1/2 t lemon extract
  • 1 t vanilla
  • 2 c. chopped pecans as garnish

Instructions

  1. Grease and flour 3x 8" cake pans.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Set aside. In mixer beat eggs until frothy. Beat in oil. Add dry mix to the wet. Blend in carrots. Pour batter into pans. Bake at 350 Degrees for 30 minutes or till toothpick comes out clean. Let cool in pan for 15 minutes before inverting onto rack. Once cooled apply frosting between layers as well as cake top and sides. Sprinkle with pecans for decorations.
