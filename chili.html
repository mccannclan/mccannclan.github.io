Chili

Do not add corn!

Ingredients

  • 2 15oz cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 15oz cans Bush's chili beans
  • 1 lb. hamburger
  • 1/2 to 1 onion
  • 1/2 to 1 green pepper
  • 3 T chili powder
  • 1 T cumin
  • dash Tabasco
  • 2 chili bean cans of water

Instructions

  1. Brown Hamubrger with onions and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce to simmer, stirring occasionally, for about an hour.
