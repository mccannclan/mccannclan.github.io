BBQ Sauce

The famous McCann BBQ sauce, first time published!

Ingredients

  • 1 /4 c vinegar
  • 1/3 c brown sugar
  • 1/3 c worcestershire sauce
  • dash tabasco
  • 1 c water
  • 1 c ketchup

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients
  2. Bring to a boil -- then simme runtil it thickens.
