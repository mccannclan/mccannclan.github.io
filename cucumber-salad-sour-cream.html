Cucumber Salad with Sour Cream

No Intro

Ingredients

  • 2 narrow (English) cucumbers
  • 1 c sour cream
  • 1/2 t salt
  • white pepper
  • 1/2 t sugar
  • 1 T finely chopped onions
  • fresh dill (optional)

Instructions

  1. For Dressing: Combine sour cream, salt, pepper, sugar, onions.
  2. Peel and slice cucumbers. Sprinkle some salt over them. Cover with ice cubes and plastic. Refrigerate one hour. Remove, drain, rinse, and pat dry. Toss gently with dressing. Refrigerate. Could sprinkle with fresh dill.
