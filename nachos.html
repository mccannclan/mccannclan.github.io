Nachos

AKA: A people pleaser!

Ingredients

  • large can (28 oz) can refried beans
  • 2 envelopes taco seasoning
  • 2 avocados, mashed
  • 1/2 c. sour cream
  • 2 small cans chopped olives
  • 1 bunch green onions, sliced
  • 2 or 3 tomatoes, sliced thin
  • cheddar cheese, grated
  • tortilla chips

Instructions

  1. 1st layer: mix refried beans and taco seasoning and spread on platter
  2. 2nd layer: mix avocados and sour cream. spread on top of first layer.
  3. 3rd layer: Put olives on next
  4. 4th layer: Now put on onions
  5. 5th Place tomatoes on top of onions
  6. 1st layer: Sprinkle the cheese on top
  7. Serve with tortilla chips
  8. Enjoy!

Notes McCormick taco seasoning is the best.
