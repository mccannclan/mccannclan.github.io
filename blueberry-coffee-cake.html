Blueberry Coffee Cake

A special treat for Christmas morning, graduation brunch or baptism brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c butter
  • 1 c sugar
  • 3 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 t baking powder
  • 1/4 t salt
  • 1 t soda
  • 2 c flour, unsifted
  • 1 c sour cream
  • 2 c fresh or frozen blueberries **
  • Topping 1 c brown sugar
  • 1/4c butter
  • 1/4 c flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, baking powder, salt, and soda. Alternating, add flour and sour cream. Fold in blueberries. Pour into a well battered cake pan approximately 9 x 13
  2. For the topping: Cream brown sugar and butter. Add flour to get a semi-dry, lump mixture. Spread on top of the batter, bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. The topping should melt and partially sink into the batter.
  3. ** Denotes the cake needs longer to cook if you use frozen blueberries. Fresh or frozen are equally delicious.

Cook Time: 30 mins
