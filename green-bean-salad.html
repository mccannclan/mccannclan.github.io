Green Bean Salad

Everyone loves this!

Ingredients

  • 2 large garlic cloves, pressed
  • 1 t salt
  • 1/4 c olive oil
  • 3 T wine vinegar (red or white)
  • 1/2 t Dijon style ground mustard
  • dash pepper
  • 1/2 tsp crumbled thyme
  • 1 lb. fresh green beans, snapped
  • 6-10 fresh mushroom caps, sliced
  • 3 stalks celery, stripped of strings, sliced thin

Instructions

  1. Put together first 7 ingredients and mix vigorously to emulsify
  2. Bring water to a boil, toss in beans, boil again, cook for 5 minutes
  3. Add beans with mushrooms and celery, to dressing
  4. Let sit at room temp for several hours

    5. Notes Use a vegetable peeler to strip celery of strings or skip this step if you're in a hurry. Follow the directions exactly for cooking the beans--they turn out perfectly.
