Two Favorite Chicken Salad Recipes from Aunt Kris

My Mom’s Standard Chicken Salad

  • 2 cups or more cooked chopped chicken (great for rotisserie chicken)
  • 1 jar olives with pimentos, drained and chopped, about a cup
  • About 1 ½ cups celery, cut in small slices
  • Mayonnaise to taste (1/2 cup or so)
  • (Optional) Italian parsley chopped ½ cup

Stir all together and serve on sandwiches or as a salad on lettuce. (It’s even better the second day!)

Curry Chicken Salad

  • 2 cups or more cooked chopped chicken
  • 1 to 1 ½ cups red grapes, halved
  • Chopped Italian parsley, about ½ cup
  • 1 to 1 ½ cups celery, cut in small slices
  • Curry powder to taste (at least a tablespoon, depends on how spicy you like curries)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mayonnaise to taste (1/2 cup or more)
  • Chopped cashews for garnish

Stir all ingredients together. When serving, garnish with chopped or whole cashews.
