Pork Chow Mein

An Irish, Asian dish :-)

Ingredients

  • 1-2 pounds pork, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 bunch of celery, sliced thinly
  • 1 onion, sliced thinly
  • 1 10 oz can sliced water chestnuts, drained
  • 1 16oz can bean sprouts, drained
  • soy sauce
  • corn starch
  • 1/2 - 1 cup water
  • 3 cups cooked brown rice

Instructions

  1. Brown pork in a little oil in a large frying pan. Remove from pan and set aside
  2. Add a little more oil and fry celery and onion until slightly soft.
  3. Add water chestnuts and bean sprouts. Return cooked pork to pan.
  4. Mi together corn starch and water. Add to ingredients in frying pan
  5. Add soy sauce to taste.
  6. Serve over rice.

    7. Total time: 45 minutes
