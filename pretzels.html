Connecticut Pretzels

With 8 children, Aunt Caroline knew how to fill up a crowd!

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg sourdough pretzels
  • 1 pkg ranch dressing
  • 1 c corn oil
  • 1 t dill weed
  • 1 t garlic powder
  • 1 t lemon pepper

Instructions

  1. Break pretzels into large pieces
  2. Mix next 5 ingredients and then pour over pretzels and mix
  3. Bake at 8 minutes at 350 degrees, remove and stir.
  4. Bake 8 minutes more.

Notes Preheat oven to 350 degrees
