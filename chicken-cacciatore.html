Chicken Cacciatore

Cook time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c flour
  • 1 t salt
  • 1/4 t pepper
  • 16 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 c mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 c water
  • 1 small can black olives
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 t oregano
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt and pepper.
  2. Coat chicken in seasoned flour.
  3. Brown chicken
  4. Mix everything together and pour over the chicken. Simmer about 30 minutes.
