Overnight Breakfast Oatmeal

Super Easy! Hot breakfast ready for you first thing int he morning. Nutritious and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 4 c water
  • 1 c steel cut oats
  • salt
  • dried fruit (optional)
  • cinnamon sugar (optional)
  • cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Put 4 cups of water in a slow cooker
  2. add 1 cup steel cut oats.
  3. add a dash of salt
  4. Add 1 cup of dried fruit
  5. Put lid on and cook on low for 7 hours.
  6. Serve with cinnamon sugar, sliced bananas, and cream!
