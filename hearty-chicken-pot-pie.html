Hearty Chicken Pot Pie

Perfect for leftover Thanksgiving and Christmas turkey and sides !

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg 16 oz frozen mixed vegetables, thawed (or the equivalent of leftover veggies)
  • 1 c cut-up cooked chicken or turkey
  • 1 can condensed cream of chicken soup (or leftover gravy)
  • 1 c original bisquick mix
  • 1/2 c milk
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Mix vegetables, chicken and soup or gravy in ungreased 2 quart casserole
  2. Stir remaining ingredients in small bowl with fork until blended. Pour into casserole dish.
  3. Bake 30 minutes or until golden brown.

    4. Prep time: 15 minutes

    Cook time: 30 minutes

    Total time: 45 minutes

    Notes: If you have a big crew after Christmas, this multiplies perfectly!
