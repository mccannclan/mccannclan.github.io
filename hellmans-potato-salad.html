Hellmann's Potato Salad

A summer staple

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. potatoes (5 or 6 medium)
  • 2 T vinegar
  • 1 t sugar
  • 1 c Hellmann's Mayonnaise
  • 1 c thinly sliced celery
  • 1/2 c chopped onion
  • 2 hard cooked eggs, chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 t ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 t salt

Instructions

  1. Cover potatoes with water in a 4-1uart sauce pot; bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer 10 minutes or until potatoes are fork-tender. Drain and cool slightly. Mix mayo, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper./li>
  2. Gently toss potatoes, mayonnaise mixture, celery, onion, and eggs. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

    3. NotesUse half a recipe for the 2 lbs of potatoes. A 5 pound bag is perfect for the complete recipe. Uncle David suggests boiling the potatoes with the skin on, and then peeling. I concur.
