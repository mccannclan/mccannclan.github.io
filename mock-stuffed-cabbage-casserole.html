Mock Stuffed Cabbage Casserole

A delightful meal-in-one that reduces all the toil of making stuffed cabbage

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 3/4 c plain raw rice
  • 1 small head green cabbage, chopped coarsely
  • 1 15oz can tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Sautee hamburger and onion until meat is done and onions are transparent. Add green pepper and cook 3 minutes more.
  2. Mix all other ingredients together. Rice will cook as dish cooks. Add meat and onion mixture. Place in casserole dish, cover and bake at 350 degrees about 1.5 hours or until most of the liquid is cooked down.

    3. Prep Time: 15 minutes

    Cook time: 1 hour 30 minutes
