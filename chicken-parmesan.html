Chicken Parmesan

Simplest Chicken parm ever! Cook time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c bread crumbs
  • 2 T olive oil
  • 1 T parmesan
  • 1 c grated mozzarella
  • 3/4 c spaghetti sauce
  • 4 chicken breasts

Instructions

  1. Spread spaghetti sauce in bottom of 9x12 pan
  2. Top with chicken breasts
  3. Mix the other ingredients and sprinkle on top of chicken
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 minutes
  5. Serve on spaghetti with extra sauce.
