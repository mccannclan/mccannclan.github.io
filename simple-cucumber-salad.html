Simple Cucumber Salad

Dorothy and Emmett Keenan were some of Grandma and Grandpa Kautz's best friends. Dorothy brought this delicious cucumber salad to mom's house, along with barbecued chicken after mom died.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cucumbers
  • 1/3 c vinegar
  • 1/3 c sugar
  • fresh dill (optional)

Instructions

  1. Slice cucumbers into thin circles. Salt lightly and place in colander for 1 hour to drain. Cover with dressing of one part vinegar, and one part sugar, cooked to dilute sugar. For 3 cucumbers, 1/3 c vinegar, 1/3 c sugar. Refrigerate at least one hour. Can sprinkle with fresh dill.
