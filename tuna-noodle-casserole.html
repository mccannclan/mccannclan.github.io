Tuna Noodle Casserole

A go-to dish for weeknight meals.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz wide egg noodles
  • 1 can chunk light tuna
  • 1/2 c mayonnaise
  • 1/2 c sour cream
  • 1/2 c milk
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 c celery, chopped
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1/4 c green pepper, chopped
  • 4 oz American cheese
  • 1/4 c slivered almonds (optional)
  • 1/4 c green olives, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the egg noodles and drain them. Mix the remaining ingredients and fold into the noodles. Place in a greased 2 quart casserole and bake uncovered at 425 degrees for 20 minutes.

    2. Notes This is very adaptable to what is in the fridge and pantry; no soup, no problem -- make your own; no noodles, use macaroni; no mayonnaise, use more sour cream, and vice versa and so on.
