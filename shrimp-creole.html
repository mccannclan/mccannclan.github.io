Shrimp Creole

With the long list of ingredients, this looks a lot more complicated than it is. Delicious and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 1 T vegetable oil
  • 1 T flour
  • 1 medium stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 med garlic clove, minced
  • 1 medium bell pepper, chopped
  • 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 2 T canned tomato paste
  • 1/4 c red wine
  • 1 c chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/8 t smoked hot paprika
  • 1/4 t dried basil
  • 1/4 t dried oregano
  • 1/4 t salt
  • 1/4 t black pepper
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 T scallions, minced
  • 1 T fresh parsley
  • 1 T fresh lemon juiced
  • 2 c cooked brown rice

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet. Stir in flour and cook over low heat until mixture is smooth, about 5 minutes. Stir in celery, onion, garlic, and pepper; sautee minutes. Add canned tomatoes, tomato paste, wine, broth, and seasonings. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low.
  2. Stir in shrimp and simmer 5 minutes. Stir in scallions, parsley and lemon juice; simmer 1 minute. To serve, spoon 1/2 c of rice into each of 4 bowls and top each with about 2 cups of shrimp mixture.

    3. Prep time:30 minutes

    Cook time:20 minutes

    Total time:50 minutes

    NotesCan use chicken instead of shrimp
