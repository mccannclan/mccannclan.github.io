Butter Horns

We had these for every holiday growing up. Light but buttery!

Ingredients

  • 1 cake yeast
  • 1 T lukewarm water
  • 1 T sugar
  • 1 c lukewarm water
  • 1/2 c melted butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 c flour

Instructions

  1. Crumble 1 cakeyeast in 1 T lukewarm water; add 1 T sugar
  2. Mix all ingredients but butter together. Cover tightly with saran wrap. Refrigerate overnight. Separate into two hunks. Roll into 1/4 inch (at most) thickness, into circular shape. Cut like pie wedges Melt some butter (about 1/4 cup); spread butter onto wedges and roll up wedges starting wiht the wide end. Allow to rise at room temperature for 2 hours. Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes or until light brown.
