Cole Slaw Dressing

Inexpensive and easy to take on a picnic. Add some crumbled blue cheese and/or crumbled bacon--sublime!

Ingredients

  • 1 c mayonnaise
  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 1/4 c vinegar
  • 2 bags shredded cabbage or one large cabbage

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients and add shredded cabbage to the dressing.
  2. Halve this recipe for a regular size cabbage head.
  3. This whole recipe is perfect for two 1 pound bags of already shredded cabbage.
