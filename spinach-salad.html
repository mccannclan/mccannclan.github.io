Spinach Salad

Classic

Ingredients

  • 5 slices bacon, cut up
  • 2 T white wine vinegar
  • 1 T lemon juice
  • 2 t sugar
  • 1/2 t salt
  • dash of pepper
  • 1/2 c sliced green onion
  • 2 hard cooked eggs, chopped
  • 10 oz pkg torn fresh spinach

Instructions

  1. Fry bacon til crisp. Remove bacon from pan. Leave 2 T bacon drippings in pan. Add vingear, lemon juice, sugar and salt and pepper. Cook about 1 minute. Pour over spinach in large salad bowl. Toss. Add bacon and eggs
