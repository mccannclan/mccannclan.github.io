"Steak" Soup

One of the Kenosha babysiting co-op moms brought this to us when Audrey was born. Quick and easy. Children love the alphabet noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground round
  • 1 16 oz can tomatoes
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 3 medium carrots, sliced
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1/3 c pearl barley
  • 1/4 c ketchup
  • 1 T beef bouillon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 t dried basil
  • alphabet noodles (optional)
  • 5 cups water

Instructions

  1. Brown meat and drain. Stir in remaining ingredients and water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 hour. Season to taste.
  2. Remove bay leaf

Notes If you have alphabet noodles, wait until the veggies are done to add them.
