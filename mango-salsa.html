Mango Salsa

Serve with a rotisserie chicken for a great no-cook meal on a hot night!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium mangoes, finely diced
  • 1 medium sweet red pepper, finely diced
  • 1/2 medium jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 T cilantro, chopped
  • 1 & 1/2 T fresh lime juice
  • 1/8 t salt
  • 1/2 t black pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
