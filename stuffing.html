Stuffing

Can be doubled. For a doubled recipe, use one 24 oz loaf of bread. Also may use chicken broth in place of some of the butter.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 c chopped onion
  • 1 & 1/2 c celery
  • 1 c margarine
  • 9 c bread cubes
  • 2 t salt
  • 1 & 1/2 t sage
  • 1 t thyme
  • 1/2 t pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook onion and celery and margarine until onion is tender. Stir in 1/2 of the bread crumbs. Dump into a bowl, add everything else and mix.
  2. If not stuffing turkey, put stuffing in casserole, drizzel about 1/2 c chicken stock on top, cover and bake for around 30 min at 350 degrees. If you want the top crunchy, remove aluminum foil and cook a little longer.
