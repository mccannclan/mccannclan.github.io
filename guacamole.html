Guacamole

Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 3 large, ripe avocados, mashed
  • 3 ounces cream cheese
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 2 oz green chilis, chopped
  • 1 T olive oil
  • 1 t red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 t salt
  • fresh ground pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients until creamy. If you are making this in advance, place it in a bowl with avocado pits to prevent discoloring. Serve with corn chips or tortilla chips.
